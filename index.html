Connection Failed

Unable to reach your one-G ATD's Instructor Operator Station.

Is the ATD powered on? The ATD must be fully powered on and its startup countdown must have finished before the IOS can accept connections.

If you're still unable to connect, follow these steps to restore connectivity:

  1. Power down the ATD.
  2. Unplug the ATD's main power cable from the wall outlet.
  3. Wait approximately 30 seconds.
  4. Plug the main power cable back into the wall outlet.
  5. Wait at least 2 minutes for the wireless router to fully boot. To confirm it's ready, check for the simCONNECT network in your smartphone's Wi-Fi list.
  6. Once simCONNECT appears, power on the ATD as normal. After the startup countdown finishes, tap Retry Connection below.

Connection Lost

Connection to your one-G ATD's Instructor Operator Station was lost.

The IOS application on the ATD may have restarted or encountered an error. The kiosk will automatically reconnect when the IOS becomes available again.

If the ATD appears to be operating normally, the IOS software may need a moment to recover. If this persists, a full ATD restart may be required:

  1. Power down the ATD.
  2. Unplug the ATD's main power cable from the wall outlet.
  3. Wait approximately 30 seconds.
  4. Plug the main power cable back into the wall outlet.
  5. Wait at least 2 minutes for the wireless router to fully boot. To confirm it's ready, check for the simCONNECT network in your smartphone's Wi-Fi list.
  6. Once simCONNECT appears, power on the ATD as normal. After the startup countdown finishes, tap Retry Connection below.

If the issue persists, contact one-G support.

1G-IOS Kiosk Launcher Information

The one-G Instructor Operator Station (IOS) is a browser-based control interface for your one-G AATD. It enables instructors to configure flight scenarios, monitor student performance, introduce system failures, and manage training sessions in real time. Session data is recorded and automatically uploaded to the one-G Portal for review and debrief.

This kiosk app connects your device directly to the IOS software running on your one-G AATD. To use it, you must be connected to the same local network as your simulator — typically, the simCONNECT Wi-Fi network that the ATD broadcasts. Refer to your documentation for connection instructions.

If you're not currently with your device, the one-G IOS Kiosk Launcher won't be able to reach your IOS. Once on-site and connected to your ATD's network, enter your tail number into the field above, and the one-G IOS Kiosk Launcher will handle the rest.
