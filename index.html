Connection Failed

Unable to reach the Instructor Operator Station. Follow these steps to restore connectivity:

  1. Power down the ATD
  2. Unplug the main ATD power cable from the wall
  3. Wait ~30 seconds
  4. Plug the main ATD power cable back into the wall
  5. IMPORTANT: Wait 2 minutes for the wireless router to fully boot (look for simCONNECT wireless network to appear on your smartphone's available network list)
  6. Only after completing step 5, proceed with powering up the ATD like normal. After the startup countdown finishes, click the button below to attempt reconnection.

If the failure persists, contact one-G support for further investigation.
